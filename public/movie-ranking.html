The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Dragon Ball Super: Broly

Favorite anime and has incredible fighting and action scenes. Also has great pacing as a majority of the movie is fighting.

Jujutsu Kaisen 0

Great plot, has great fight scenes and the prequel is necessary to appreciate Season 2 of Jujutsu Kaisen. Also Yuta's story is finally revealed after being referenced in Season 1.

Avengers: Endgame

Loved how it completes Avenger: Infinity War. Also had an incredible war that was like nothing I've seen before. Thanos is my favorite villain in the Marvel universe.
